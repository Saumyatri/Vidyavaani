RESOURCES TO BROWSE

There are many virtual free resources available to educate children and adolescents, including but not limited to:


Kode With Klossy

A 2 week summer programme for female identifying individuals with courses on Website Development, Mobile App Development, Data Science and Artifical Intelligence.

Khan Academy

A website offering practice exercises, instructional videos, and a personalized learning dashboard that empowers learners to study maths, science, computing, history, economics,etc at their own pace in and outside of the classroom.

3 Blue 1 Brown

A Math YouTube channel created and run by Grant Sanderson focused on teaching higher mathematics from a visual perspective, and on the process of discovery and inquiry-based learning in mathematics.

Wait But Why

A website that covers a variety of topics with interesting and entertaining methods and a few doodles. From Artificial Intelligence to paradoxes and history, Wait But Why seeks to answer questions of all kinds that we can come up with.

Explorable Explanations

An 'experiment' webpage that consists of interactive elements that can be used to learn concepts across STEM fields. The page has approximately 180 explorable interactive elements and articles/talks about explorable explanations.
